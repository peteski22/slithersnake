🎙️ Tab audio recorder

Captures the audio of a browser tab to a file, so we can turn snake.io's sounds into spectrograms and base ours on them.

  1. Open snake.io in another tab and start a game. Turn its music OFF in settings so only sound effects play.
  2. Click Start recording below. In the picker, choose the snake.io tab and tick “Share tab audio”.
  3. Switch to snake.io and trigger each effect once (eat a pellet, boost, die). Pauses between them help.
  4. Come back here and click Stop — a .webm file downloads.
  5. Move it into .superpowers/refs/audio/ and run the spectrogram command shown after you stop.
